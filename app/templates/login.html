Welcome to the Search Ranking Survey

In this course, the recordings of lectures have been analyzed using artificial intelligence to allow students to search for terms and phrases from lecture recordings. This research is funded by the University of Michigan and the National Science Foundation.

In this survey we simulate a search for a particular topic on each page. Per topic, four videos are presented along with a snippet of the text being spoken at the timestamp in the video returned by the search. We ask you to identify which of the four video clips is the most relevant clip if you were searching for the topic identified on the page. We also ask you to identify which of the four video clips is the most irrelevant clip if you were searching for the topic identified on the page.

To receive common good credit, please review each of the four clips on each page, indicate which video clip is the most relevant to the search topic, and identify which video clip is the most irrelevant to the search topic. The results will be used to improve the search engine in the future.

Thank you for your participation in this survey.
- Prof. Samson
